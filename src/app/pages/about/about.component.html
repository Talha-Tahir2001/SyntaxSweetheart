❤️ Frequently Asked Questions

💌 How does the AI generate letters?

Our AI analyzes your input using natural language processing and romantic pattern recognition to craft unique, heartfelt messages. 💖

🔒 Is my data private?

Absolutely! 🛡️ We don’t store any personal data or letter content. Your love letters remain yours forever. ❤️

🎭 Can I customize the tone of the letter?

Yes! 🌹 Choose from romantic, playful, heartfelt, or poetic styles to match your special moment. ✨

💲 Is the service free to use?

Yes! 💕 The basic version is free, and we offer premium features for even more personalization. 🌟

🌍 Can I generate letters in multiple languages?

Absolutely! 🗣️ Our AI speaks the language of love in multiple languages. 💕

✍️ Can I edit the generated letter?

Yes! 📝 After generation, you can tweak and personalize it to make it even more special. 💕

🆓 Do I need an account to use the service?

No! 🚀 You can generate letters instantly. But creating an account unlocks extra features! 🔓

❤️ How can I ensure the letter reflects my relationship?

By sharing details like shared memories and dreams, our AI personalizes your letter to perfection. 💑

🎉 Can I use the generated letters for other occasions?

Definitely! 💌 Write letters for anniversaries, birthdays, or even just to brighten someone's day! ☀️

📝 Is there a word limit for the letters?

No strict limit! 💕 However, concise inputs generate the best heartfelt results. ✨
